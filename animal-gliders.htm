Animal Gliders

Embark on a Thrilling, High-Flying Journey!

Embark on a thrilling, high-flying journey with Animal Gliders, the exhilarating 2D endless mobile game where you become one of ten unique animals, each with their own special abilities. Imagine hurtling down a towering mountain, navigating through a perilous, ever-changing landscape filled with obstacles that can spell doom at any moment.

In this adrenaline-pumping adventure, you must deftly maneuver your chosen animal through a series of deadly traps, treacherous cliffs, and unexpected hazards. But it's not all doom and gloom, strategically placed power-ups and items can give you a much-needed boost or throw a wrench in your plans, adding an extra layer of excitement and unpredictability to your descent.

Animal Gliders combines the fast-paced, endless fun of games like Jetpack Joyride and Doodle Jump with the unique challenge of navigating a vertical world where gravity is both your friend and your foe. Master the art of gliding, dodge obstacles with precision, and see how far you can fall in this endlessly entertaining, beautifully animated adventure. How long can you survive the ultimate mountain descent? Play now and find out.
