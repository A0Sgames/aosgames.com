Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

We collect information to enhance your gaming experience. This includes:

  • User-Provided Data: Information you directly provide when setting up your game or making in-game purchases.
  • Device Storage Access: Our game requires access to your device's storage to save game progress and settings.
  • Internet Connectivity: Certain features of the game, such as playability, leaderboards, and updates, require an internet connection. The game can not be played without an internet connection.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Store your game progress, preferences, and in-game achievements.
  • Enable future features such as cloud saves, leaderboards, and social sharing.
  • Provide updates, new content, and enhancements to the game.
  • Assist with customer support inquiries and troubleshoot issues.

Sharing Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users and the public.
  • With third-party services that facilitate specific in-game features (e.g., cloud saves or multiplayer servers).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information stored in our systems.
  • Withdraw permissions for accessing device storage or the internet, understanding that certain features may be affected.

Contact us at support@aosgames.com to exercise your rights or for further assistance.

Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of data transmission or storage is entirely secure.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and significant changes will be communicated to you through the game.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@aosgames.com.
